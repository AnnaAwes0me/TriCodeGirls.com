Welcome to TriCode Girls!


What is Tricode girls?

Tricode girls is a coding club centered on teaching and supporting girls of all ages, all ethnicities,
and all backgrounds. Girls will build skills in cybersecurity, computer science, and programming.
Our club is and always will be completely free. We believe that money should not be the object that stands
between a girl and her interests or dreams. Tricode girls club has classes in two age groups; Middle School
and High-school, the main difference being the High school group will include training for the girls
who choose to participate in the Girls Go Cyberstart competition team.

Our Founder

Anna Lorberblatt is a student at Western Carolina University where she is working towards a dual
major in environmental science and computer programming. Outside of school, she enjoys reading, writing,
camping, and spending time with her friends. Anna frequently volunteers through various organizations
and in a special needs ministry. She works to encourage and empower women in STEM and looks forward
to a future where computer science isn’t considered a “man’s field.”
