Welcome to Tri Code Girls!


Who are we?

We are Raleigh-native Girls who operate under Generation's homeschool group.
We consist of a High-school age group and a middle School age group, with ages ranging
from 9 to 18. Our goal is to serve our community by introducing girls to coding concepts at young ages.

Our story

Our Founder

Anna Lorberblatt is a student at Western Carolina University where she is working towards a dual
major in environmental science and computer programming. Outside of school, she enjoys reading, writing,
camping, and spending time with her friends. Anna frequently volunteers through various organizations
and in a special needs ministry. Anna works to encourage and empower women in STEM and looks forward
to a future where computer science isn’t considered a “man’s field.”
