Middle School Girls


Classes

During the course of Tricode girls club middle school girls will work
and learn together as we explore lessons in cryptography, programming,
web design, and hacking. Tricode girls also offers a small library of
books on coding or famous historical women in STEM. We also have some
“library computers” that we loan out to girls who don’t have a computer
to use for our classes. We hope to encourage girls to think outside
of the box and pursue interests in otherwise male dominated fields.
