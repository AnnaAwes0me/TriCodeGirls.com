Middle School Girls


Classes

The middle school branch of Tricode girls club is taught by Zaia,
using curriculum from the Girls Who Code franchise. Early lessons
will focus on computer programming basics before moving to lessons
on cybersecurity, python, html and css based web pages, and eventually
cover cryptography and ethical hacking. Each individual girl will gain
a number of computer skills as well as have access to our small library of books about coding and stories of historical women in stem
.
