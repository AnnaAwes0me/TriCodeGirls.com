Welcome to Tri Code Girls!

Who are we?

We are a Raleigh-native girls only coding club who operate under Generation's homeschool
group. We consist of a High-school age group and a middle School age group,
with ages ranging from 9 to 18. Our goal is to serve our community by
introducing girls to coding concepts at young ages.
